# Rankings — Top 50 (Editorial)

Editorial top picks. Curated by [@prompt-librarian](https://github.com/frankxai/prompt-engine/blob/main/agents/prompt-librarian.md).

Editorial selection considers:
- **Eval score** (must be >= 4.0/5.0).
- **Coverage** — does this pattern fill a category that's otherwise sparse?
- **Cross-lab portability** — does it work well across labs?
- **Provenance health** — is the attribution clean and the license clear?
- **Real-world usage signal** — has the community used or remixed it?

Unlike `by-eval-score.md`, this ranking is human-curated and may surface patterns with lower eval scores when they fill a gap or demonstrate technique.

## How this list is maintained

`@prompt-librarian` reviews the corpus on a rolling basis. When a new pattern lands with strong eval + clear gap-fill, it's considered for top-50 inclusion. Existing entries may be demoted if a better variant emerges.

Cadence: review monthly, refresh quarterly.
