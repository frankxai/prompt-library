# Examples — analyze_claims

## Example 1
**INPUT**: "Remote work makes people lazier. Within five years, most knowledge workers will return to offices. The data is obvious."

**OUTPUT** (excerpt):
## FACTUAL CLAIMS
1. "Remote work makes people lazier." Testability 6/10 — requires productivity proxy + control group.
2. "The data is obvious." Testability 8/10 — name the data, check the citation.
## OPINION CLAIMS
- (none clearly bounded)
## PREDICTION CLAIMS
1. "Most knowledge workers will return to offices within five years." Specificity moderate.
## RHETORICAL DEVICES
- "The data is obvious" appeals to authority without naming the data.
