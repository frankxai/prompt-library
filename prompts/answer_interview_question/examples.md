# Examples — answer_interview_question

## Example 1
**QUESTION**: "Tell me about a time you had to influence without authority."
**CANDIDATE_CONTEXT**: PM at SaaS, drove cross-team migration without budget control.

**OUTPUT**:
Last year our API gateway was becoming a bottleneck — three teams depended on it but none owned migration. I had no authority over those teams. I started by writing a one-page document showing each team's specific cost of inaction with their own metrics. Then I scheduled fifteen-minute one-on-ones with each tech lead, listened first, and asked what would make the migration low-risk for them. Within four weeks all three teams committed to a shared timeline. We shipped the migration two months later with zero downtime. The lesson I took: influence without authority runs on translated incentives, not on persuasion.
