# Examples — check_grammar

## Example 1
**INPUT**: "Their going to the store, its closing at 9pm. Me and him will buy snacks."

**OUTPUT** (excerpt):
## ERRORS
1. Error: "Their" → Correction: "They're" — Reason: contraction of "they are", not possessive.
2. Error: "its" → Correction: "it's" — Reason: contraction of "it is", not possessive.
3. Error: "Me and him will buy" → Correction: "He and I will buy" — Reason: subject pronouns required for compound subject.
## CORRECTED VERSION
They're going to the store; it's closing at 9pm. He and I will buy snacks.
