# Examples — create_quiz

## Example 1 — Photosynthesis primer
**INPUT**: "Photosynthesis converts light energy, water, and CO2 into glucose and oxygen, in chloroplasts."

**OUTPUT** (excerpt):
## QUESTIONS
1. [MCQ] Where does photosynthesis happen? a) mitochondria b) chloroplasts c) ribosomes d) nucleus
2. [Short] Name the two inputs other than light.
3. [Application] A plant in low-light conditions for a week shows yellowing. Explain why in two sentences.

## ANSWER KEY
1. b) chloroplasts — site of chlorophyll and light reactions.
2. Water and CO2.
3. Reduced light reduces glucose production; chlorophyll degrades without light cue, causing yellowing.
