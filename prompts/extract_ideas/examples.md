# Examples — extract_ideas

## Example 1
**INPUT**: "Reading 200 books changes the reader more than the books change. The 200th book teaches more than the third because the reader has a richer substrate."

**OUTPUT**:
## IDEAS
- Reading volume changes the reader more than each book changes.
- The 200th book teaches more than the third because the reader has changed.
- A reader's prior reading determines which books read as profound.
