# Examples — extract_insights

## Example 1
**INPUT**: "Three founders all said the same thing — they didn't quit when the data was bad, they quit when the people were."

**OUTPUT**:
## INSIGHTS
- Founders measure their own commitment through the people around them, not the metrics.
- Quitting signals are interpersonal long before they are financial.
- Repeated convergent stories from independent operators are stronger signal than any single one.
